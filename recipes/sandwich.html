How to make a simple Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 toasted/non-toasted bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cheese slice
  • 1 tbsp canola oil

    • and it is up to you to decide whether or not you should add more toppings! eg. Ham, lettuce, etc. Anything that you would think be the perfect topping for your delicious and simple sandwich.

Procedure:

  1. Heat the pan in medium temperature and pour the oil
  2. wait for 1 minute then crack the egg into the pan and wait for 2 minutes
  3. while waiting put 2 toasted/non-toasted bread in a plate, prioritize the other toppings first before putting on the cheese slice
  4. lastly, use the spatula to grab the egg and put it on top of the cheese slice then serve!
